About Me


I am a front-end focused web developer with professional training in music. With my background in music, I am prepared to think critically about solving problems, and I am capable of quickly learning new skills and working hard to meet deadlines. I look forward to sharing my talents in a friendly and productive environment and being hands on in creating new applications and websites.
